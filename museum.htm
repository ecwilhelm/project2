Program Cover & Web Banner

design

    • Web banner for the Association of Midwest Museums Annual Conference
    • The program cover and matching web banner for the annual conference of the Association of Midwest Museums (AMM) were created in InDesign as a group project for a document design course.
    • The association requested a colorful, modern design representing the variety of member museums. They provided various images of museums in Cleveland, where the annual conference was taking place, so we combined them into a mosaik and shaped it to a usb cable.
    • This mosaik object illustrated the conference slogan "Museums Making Connections" and provided a colorful focal point for our designs. To showcase the mosaik graphic, the rest of the design is kept clean and uncluttered. The color scheme with purple and organge accents colors was derived from the colors of the hosting Ohio Museums Association's logo that was a required element on the cover.
    • The set of promotional objects consisted of a program cover (both in color and black and white versions) to be produced in print and a banner for web advertisement.
