    • Screenshot of the Albstadt-Sigmaringen University publication
    • While working as e-learning coordinator at the newly established Media Department at Albstadt-Sigmaringen University (Germany), I was responsible for all department communications. The German version of this article, Wissen per Mausklick, was originally published in the annual university publication in 2009.
    • Since the Media Department had just been established, its existence and purpose had not yet been broadly publicized within the university community. The article was intended to introduce university stakeholders to e-learning in general and to the e-learning activities at Albstadt-Sigmaringen University at that time.
    • Consulting faculty about the use of e-learning in teaching, maintaining the learning management system and other learning technologies, and providing support for faculty, staff and students were the main responsibilities of the Media Department. The article was a means of introducing the department and its services. Responses to the article were extremely positive, and the number of service requests increased in the weeks following the publication.
